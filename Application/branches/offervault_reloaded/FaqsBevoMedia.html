Frequently Asked Questions

What is BeVo Media?

BeVo Media is an Internet ad network consolidation platform. Our goal is to simplify our publishers efforts and educate them as much as possible to help them grow and ultimately generate more revenue. By supplying a variety of features, such as our Classroom, Keyword Tracker, Website Analytics, and PPC Expense Consolidation, a Super Campaign Editor, A Full Marketplace, and Classroom, BeVo Media is the only place you'll ever need to go to satisfy your affiliate marketing needs.

Are you an affiliate network?

No. BeVo Media manages publishers Affiliate, PPC and Analytics accounts on the BeVo interface so publishers no longer need to login to several different accounts to manage their campaigns. BeVo Media also offers numerous tools to analyze PPC expenses, web analytics, and offer revenue.

How Do you chose your affiliate networks?

We chose our affiliate networks based on their trust and reputation within the industry.

Can you help me get into some ad networks?

Yes, although there is no guarantee, BeVo maintains a strong relationship with all of our partnered networks, which enables us to pull some strings for our publishers. Please contact your mentor for more information regarding getting into networks.

I am having a problem with one of my networks. What should I do?

BeVo Media has no influence over your networks stats or payments. We are strictly independent from all affiliate networks. All questions about your account with a specific network are to between you and the corresponding network.

What is a mentor's responsibilities?

Every BeVo account is assigned an individual mentor. A publisher is free to ask his or her mentor any questions they may have about affiliate marketing or their Bevo Media account. Mentors are here to help their publishers, in any which way possible.

I am an merchant trying to advertise. Can you help me get promoted?

Sure thing! We have some of the best contacts in the internet marketing industry to get you full exposure for your site. We also may offer specific onsite advertising within the Bevo interface. Please contact advertising@bevomedia.com for more information.

I am an affiliate network, and would like to be included in the BeVo Media Exchange. What should I do?

Email contact@bevomedia.com for more information about being included on our interface.
